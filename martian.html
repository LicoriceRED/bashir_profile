The origin of how I got into coding was my interest creating tools to help me as a camera technitian in the film industry.

My major project for 2 years was slowly builting a laser and ultrasonic rangefinder system for tracking actors on screen. this tool helps with always keeping the subject in focus at all times with a live feed going wirelessly to the focus puller.

Since then it has been used on filmsets all over Australia.
